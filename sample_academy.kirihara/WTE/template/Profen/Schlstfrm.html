Schedule

Check the boxes for the times you can give lessons. You can check all boxes for the day by checking the boxes on the left side of the date.To cancel, click the time slot.

IMPORTANT! The timetable below is on a JST (Japan Standard Time) basis. Therefore, before checking boxes, you must carefully check and confirm the time difference between Japan and your location. We will not accept responsibility for damages, loss or claims of any kind caused by a misunderstanding about the lesson start time between you and the student.Note: In Japan there is no summer time.
