# 英検2級 長文の内容一致選択 問題・解説集

本ドキュメントは `seed_reading_long_content_2kyu_50.sql` に登録された長文内容一致問題（約48問）の解説です。

## 英検2級 長文の内容一致のレベル・出題傾向

### 形式（本番試験）

- **大問3**: 長文の内容一致選択 … **全8問**
- **3A**: Eメール（英文A）… 1題・**3問**（24–26）
- **3B**: 説明文（英文B）… 1題・**5問**（27–31）
- 目標解答時間：約22分

### レベル

- **対象**: 高校卒業程度（CEFR B1前後、約5,000語レベル）
- **語彙・文法**: 社会生活に必要な英語。環境・科学・健康・ビジネス・歴史・文化など多様なテーマ。
- **パッセージ語数**: Eメール約**240語**、説明文約**350語**。300～400語レベルの長文に慣れておくことが推奨される。

### 出題傾向のポイント

| タイプ | 内容 | 問い方の傾向 |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **Eメール** | 社内連絡、研修・コース案内、学校図書館・生徒会、退職パーティー・IT依頼、ワークショップ確認、会社方針、イベント支援依頼など | **Why does 〜 want 〜 to do?** / **The schedule will be changed because** / **What is one thing 〜 says about 〜?** / **By when 〜?** |
| **説明文** | 歴史・発明（紅茶パック、自転車）、生物・科学（ミラーテスト、蜜蜂）、社会・規制（広告規制）、健康（睡眠）など | **Why was 〜 surprised?** / **〜 was mainly influenced by** / **〜 were introduced to** / **Which of the following statements is true?** / **What do we learn from the passage?** |

- 設問には「内容に関する質問に適切な選択肢を選ぶ形式」と「英文を完成させる形式」の両方がある。
- 選択肢は本文の言い換えや誤った数字・因果が混ざるため、該当箇所を正確に押さえることが重要。

## 3B 説明文（Passage 7–12、各5問）

### Passage 7: History of Tea Bags

**問19** Why was Thomas Sullivan surprised at his customers?  
**正解**: They used the tea leaves in the bags just as they were delivered.  
**解説**: "To his surprise, his customers found it more convenient to pour hot water over the tea leaves while they were still in the bag. They used the bags just as they were delivered, instead of opening them." が根拠です。

**問20** The development of tea bag materials was mainly influenced by  
**正解**: the need for a large amount of production of tea bags.  
**解説**: "As tea bags became more popular, hand-making them could not meet the demand. Filter paper was introduced... and was suitable for large-scale production." が根拠です。

**問21** Why did tea bags finally become popular in Britain?  
**正解**: They fit well with the pace of modern life of British people.  
**解説**: "After World War II, however, lifestyles became busier... Tea bags fit well with the pace of modern life and finally became accepted." が根拠です。

**問22** Pyramid-shaped tea bags were introduced to  
**正解**: give more space for tea leaves to move around in tea bags.  
**解説**: "In the late 1990s, pyramid-shaped tea bags appeared. They gave more space for tea leaves to move around, which improved the taste." が根拠です。

**問23** Which of the following statements is true?  
**正解**: The materials used for tea bags are becoming more eco-friendly.  
**解説**: "Recently, there has been a move towards using paper, cloth, or silk instead of nylon or materials containing plastic, in line with the global trend to reduce plastic use" が根拠です。

### Passage 8: The Mirror Test

**問24** The mirror test is used  
**正解**: to determine if an animal can recognize itself.  
**解説**: "The mirror test is a method used to find out if an animal can recognize itself." が根拠です。

**問25** How did the cleaner wrasses behave when they were first shown a mirror?  
**正解**: They attacked their reflections, thinking they were other fish.  
**解説**: "At first, the fish attacked their reflections, perhaps thinking they were other fish." が根拠です。

**問26** What did the cleaner wrasses do when they were shown a picture of themselves with a mark?  
**正解**: They tried to get rid of the mark on their body.  
**解説**: "They tried to remove marks from pictures of themselves but not from pictures of other marked fish." が根拠です。

**問27** In the discussion about the mirror test, it has been pointed out that  
**正解**: it needs to consider the other senses that animals have.  
**解説**: "vision is not the main sense for all animals. Some rely more on smell or other senses. The test may not be suitable for every species." が根拠です。

**問28** Which of the following statements is true?  
**正解**: Some fish can understand the difference between themselves and others.  
**解説**: 写真実験で "They tried to remove marks from pictures of themselves but not from pictures of other marked fish. This suggests they can tell the difference between themselves and others." が根拠です。

## まとめ

- **3A（Eメール）**: **目的・理由・スケジュール変更・依頼内容・期限・条件**を押さえる。社内・学校・イベントなど実務的な文脈が中心。
- **3B（説明文）**: **事実・理由（Why / because）・経緯・具体例・議論・Which is true? / What do we learn?** を段落ごとに追う。環境・生物・歴史・健康・社会など2級頻出テーマを反映している。
- 本番同様、Eメール約240語・説明文約350語を目安に作成している。
