本ドキュメントは `seed_reading_long_content_jun1kyu_50.sql` に登録された長文内容一致問題（50問）の解説です。

## 英検準1級 長文の内容一致のレベル・出題傾向

### 形式（本番試験）

- **大問3**: 内容一致選択問題 … **全7問**
- **1題目**: 約**400語**の長文・**3問**
- **2題目**: 約**500語**の長文・**4問**
- 目標解答時間：約25分

### レベル

- **対象**: 大学中級程度（CEFR B2、約7,500語レベル）
- **語彙・文法**: 社会生活で求められる英語に加え、学術的・抽象的表現。約5,000語ファミリーでカバー率98%程度。
- **パッセージ語数**: 約400〜500語。正確な読解と段落ごとの要点把握が求められる。

### 出題傾向のポイント

| タイプ | 内容 | 問い方の傾向 |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **質問型** | 長文の内容に関する質問に、最も適切な選択肢を選ぶ | **According to the first paragraph, ...** / **What did X conclude?** / **Which of the following is true?** |
| **文完成型** | 英文が途中まで提示され、続く適切な選択肢で文を完成させる | **The author suggests that ...** / **Research has shown that ...** |
| **段落指定** | 特定の段落に基づく設問 | **According to the fourth paragraph, ...** / **In the final paragraph, ...** |

- **テーマ**: 社会問題・環境・自然・科学・歴史・医療・心理・技術・ビジネス・政治など。アメリカ関連の話題が多く、時事・歴史・学術的な論説文や説明文が中心。
- **選択肢**: 本文の言い換え・細部の正確な一致が求められる。紛らわしい選択肢では「段落・人物・因果」を正確に照合することが重要。

## Passage 1: The Franklin Expedition

**問1** According to the first paragraph, what was the goal of the Franklin Expedition?  
**正解**: To discover a new route connecting the Atlantic and Pacific through the Arctic.  
**解説**: 第1段落の "in search of the Northwest Passage, a sea route through the Arctic that would connect the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans" が根拠です。

**問2** What did the note discovered in 1859 reveal?  
**正解**: The ships had become trapped in ice and Franklin had died; the crew had abandoned the vessels.  
**解説**: 第2段落の "In 1859, a note was discovered... It stated that the ships had become trapped in ice and that Franklin had died in June 1847. The surviving crew had abandoned the vessels and set out on foot" が根拠です。

**問3** According to the passage, what is one cause of the crew's deaths that was identified by later investigations?  
**正解**: Lead poisoning from the solder in food tins.  
**解説**: "Later investigations revealed that many of the crew had died from lead poisoning, likely caused by the solder used to seal the tins that held their food" が根拠です。

**問4** What does the author of the passage say about the discovery of the shipwrecks?  
**正解**: The Erebus and the Terror were located on the seabed in 2014 and 2016.  
**解説**: "In 2014 and 2016, the wrecks of the Erebus and the Terror were finally located on the seabed" が根拠です。

**問5** According to the final paragraph, why do scientists and historians believe the expedition failed?  
**正解**: A combination of inadequate Arctic knowledge, outdated maps, and technology that was not equal to the challenge.  
**解説**: "Scientists and historians have argued that the expedition's failure was due to a combination of factors: inadequate knowledge of Arctic conditions, reliance on outdated maps, and the harsh reality that nineteenth-century technology was simply not equal to the challenge" が根拠です。

## Passage 2: Sleep and Memory

**問6** According to the first paragraph, what do studies show about sleep and learning?  
**正解**: People who sleep after learning new material retain it better than those who stay awake.  
**解説**: "Studies have shown that people who sleep after learning new material retain it better than those who stay awake for the same period" が根拠です。

**正解**: The replay of neural patterns that occurred during learning.  
**解説**: "One of the key mechanisms... is the replay of neural patterns during sleep" および "the brain appears to 'replay' the same patterns of activity that occurred while the person was awake and learning" が根拠です。

**問8** What have some researchers suggested about dreams?  
**正解**: They may be a by-product of the brain's consolidation process.  
**解説**: "Some researchers have suggested that dreams may be a by-product of this consolidation process" が根拠です。

**問9** According to the passage, lack of sleep has been linked to  
**正解**: poorer decision-making, increased risk of accidents, and a higher likelihood of certain health problems.  
**解説**: "Lack of sleep has been linked to poorer decision-making, increased risk of accidents, and a higher likelihood of developing certain health problems" が根拠です。

**問10** What do experts argue in the final paragraph?  
**正解**: Recognizing sleep as a fundamental component of learning and health could lead to improvements in well-being and productivity.  
**解説**: "Experts argue that recognizing sleep as a fundamental component of learning and health... could lead to significant improvements in both individual well-being and societal productivity" が根拠です。
