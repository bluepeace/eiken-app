本ドキュメントは `seed_reading_long_content_jun2kyuplus_50.sql` に登録された長文内容一致問題（約53問）の解説です。

## 英検準2級プラス 長文の内容一致のレベル・出題傾向

### 形式（本番試験）

- **大問3**: 長文の内容一致選択 … **全8問**（2級と同じ構成）
- **3A**: Eメール（英文A）… 1題・**3問**（24–26）
- **3B**: 説明文（英文B）… 1題・**5問**（27–31）

### レベル

- **対象**: 高校2年生程度（準2級〜2級の中間、約3,000語レベル）
- **語彙・文法**: 社会的な話題が中心。準2級よりやや難しく、2級より数十語短めのパッセージ。
- **パッセージ語数**: Eメール約200語、説明文約320語（2級は350語程度）。

### 出題傾向のポイント

| タイプ | 内容 | 問い方の傾向 |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **Eメール** | 学校祭スピーチ招待、作文コンクール、ホテル予約、雑誌寄稿依頼、ボランティア、インターン採用など | **What is true about 〜?** / **Why did 〜 send 〜?** / **One of the reasons 〜** / **What did 〜 ask 〜 to do?** |
| **説明文** | 環境・気候（ホワイトベア、グレートグリーンウォール）、新技術・社会（ビーンレスコーヒー）、都市・地域（街の木、地産地消、海洋プラスチック、風力） | **What is true about 〜?** / **〜 is important because** / **During the study, 〜** / **What could happen if 〜?** / **What do we learn from the passage?** |

- 選択肢は本文の言い換えや誤った数字・因果が混ざるため、該当箇所を正確に押さえることが重要。
- 過去問では、Eメール3問・説明文5問のバランスで作成しています。

## 3A Eメール（Passage 1–6、各3問）

### Passage 1: Speech at school festival

**問1** What is true about Heidi Collins?  
**正解**: She went to the same high school as Harvey Rogers.  
**解説**: 本文 "a professional tennis player who graduated from our high school" より、Heidi は Harvey と同じ Eagle Lake High School の卒業生です。

**問2** Heidi told Harvey that she would talk about  
**正解**: how she lived a professional athlete's life.  
**解説**: "You already told us that you were thinking of talking about your life as a professional athlete." が根拠です。

**問3** What will Heidi do on the day of the event?  
**正解**: Take pictures with students at the event.  
**解説**: "If possible, we would also like to take pictures of you with students." より、当日の予定の一つとして生徒との写真撮影が含まれています。

### Passage 2: Writing contest

**解説**: "I am pleased to inform you that your essay about 'life goals' has won first prize in the high school category." が手紙の主な目的です。

**問5** One of the reasons Maria Stone's essay was chosen is that  
**正解**: reading her essay would positively influence others.

**問6** What did Jacob Francis ask Maria Stone to do?  
**正解**: Let people hear her speech at the city event.  
**解説**: "We would like to ask you to read your essay as a speech at an upcoming city event next month." が依頼内容です。

## 3B 説明文（Passage 7–13、各5問）

### Passage 7: Polar Bears

**問19** What is true about global warming in the Arctic?  
**正解**: The impact is much greater than in other parts of the planet.  
**解説**: "the Arctic has been warming more than twice as fast as the world average" が根拠です。

**問20** Ice is important for polar bears because  
**正解**: living on the ice makes it easier for them to catch their food.  
**解説**: "They usually live on ice and catch animals there." および陸にいると "they try to eat things like berries, but they lose weight quickly" より、氷上で獲物を捕ることが重要です。

**問21** During the study of polar bears in summer, most polar bears  
**正解**: took action to find something to eat.  
**解説**: "Some of them rested to save energy, but most tried to find food." が根拠です。

**問22** What could happen if greenhouse gases are not reduced?  
**正解**: Most polar bears could be completely gone by 2100.  
**解説**: "Studies suggest that polar bears could struggle to exist until 2100 if greenhouse gases are not reduced." および "In some areas, polar bears may not survive beyond 2030." が根拠です。

**問23** What do we learn from the passage?  
**正解**: Polar bears lose weight if they cannot find their food on the ice.  
**解説**: 陸では "they try to eat things like berries, but they lose weight quickly" とあり、氷上で獲物を取れないと体重が減るという内容が本文の学びです。

### Passage 8: A Big Project in Africa

**問24** What is true about the Great Green Wall project?  
**正解**: It is a project that can change the condition of the soil in African countries.  
**解説**: "The project aims to improve the condition of the soil, help farmers grow more crops, boost economies, and improve the quality of life for local people." が根拠です。

**問25** How did the area around the Sahara Desert get damaged?  
**正解**: Both natural events and human activities destroyed the environment.  
**解説**: "The area... has been damaged by climate change, terrible weather events, and human activities such as repeated farming." が根拠です。

**問26** In the country of Senegal,  
**正解**: people have planted trees whose fruits can improve their physical condition.  
**解説**: "In Senegal, trees that were planted produce fruits that are good for people's health." が根拠です。

**問27** What is one of the key points to making the Great Green Wall project successful?  
**正解**: Increasing the number of jobs for the local people.  
**解説**: "Second, the project creates jobs so that local people can earn more money. This is one of the main goals" が根拠です。

**問28** What do we learn from the passage?  
**正解**: The Great Green Wall project includes caring for trees well after planting them.  
**解説**: "First, local people must take good care of the trees after they are planted." が成功の鍵の一つとして述べられています。

### Passage 9: A New Type of Coffee

**問29** What is true about the American coffee company?  
**正解**: It is one of several companies in the beanless coffee industry.  
**解説**: "The American company is not alone. Other companies in Singapore and the Netherlands are also making beanless coffee" が根拠です。

**問30** To create a taste similar to real coffee beans,  
**正解**: the American company takes advantage of many kinds of plant seeds.  
**解説**: "The American company uses plant seeds and other raw materials to create flavors that are similar to traditional coffee." が根拠です。

**問31** One of the positive features of beanless coffee is that  
**正解**: it can be more eco-friendly than traditional coffee.  
**解説**: "Traditional coffee production often leads to deforestation... Beanless coffee uses less water and fewer forests are cut down." が根拠です。

**問32** What is one of the challenges that the coffee industry faces?  
**正解**: Coffee farmers' businesses could get badly damaged.  
**解説**: "If beanless coffee replaces traditional coffee, the income of coffee farmers could decrease." が根拠です。

**問33** What do we learn from the passage?  
**正解**: It will be possible to get beanless coffee at a lower price than traditional coffee.  
**解説**: "As the price of traditional coffee rises, beanless coffee is expected to become available at a lower price as the market grows." が根拠です。

## まとめ

- **3A（Eメール）**: **送信の目的・What is true about 〜・依頼・理由・期限**を押さえる。選択肢の言い換えや別の数字に注意。
- **3B（説明文）**: **事実・理由（〜because）・研究・条件（What could happen if 〜）・主旨（What do we learn?）**を段落ごとに追う。準2級より語数が多く、社会的・環境的な話題が中心。
