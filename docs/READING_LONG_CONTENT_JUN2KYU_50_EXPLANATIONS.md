本ドキュメントは `seed_reading_long_content_jun2kyu_50.sql` に登録された長文内容一致問題（約52問）の解説です。

## 英検準2級 長文の内容一致のレベル・出題傾向

### 形式（本番試験）

- **大問4**: 長文の内容一致選択 … **全7問**
- **4A**: Eメール … 1題・**3問**（23–25）
- **4B**: 説明文（長文）… 1題・**4問**（26–29）

### レベル

- **対象**: 高校1年生程度（中学〜高校1年、約2,000語レベル）
- **語彙・文法**: 身近な話題から社会・環境・文化・歴史まで。接続詞、関係詞、不定詞・動名詞、受け身、完了形など。
- **パッセージ語数**: Eメール約200語、説明文240〜350語。段落ごとに1問出る傾向。

### 出題傾向のポイント

| タイプ | 内容 | 問い方の傾向 |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **Eメール** | 学生の依頼（インタビュー・発表）、大会・キャンプ・予約の連絡、就職・アルバイトなど | **連絡の経路・目的・依頼内容・理由・期限・代替案**。What is true? / Why ...? / What does A suggest? |
| **説明文** | 環境・生物、地域・文化、歴史・人物、科学・社会 | **事実の直接 recall**、**理由（Why）**、**段落・全体の主旨**。Which is correct? / In ..., （文完成） |

- 選択肢は本文の**言い換え**や**誤った数字・時期・因果**が混ざるため、該当箇所を正確に押さえることが重要。
- 過去3回分の過去問では、4A Eメール3問・4B 説明文4問のバランスで作成しています。

## 4A Eメール（Passage 1–8、各3問）

### Passage 1: An interview（博物館インタビュー依頼）

**問1** How was Todd able to contact Ms. Parker?  
**正解**: By getting her contact information from his teacher.  
**解説**: 本文 "My social studies teacher, Mr. Garcia, gave me your e-mail address." が根拠です。

**問2** What is one thing Todd wants to ask about the museum?  
**正解**: The efforts that the museum makes to attract visitors.  
**解説**: "I would like to ask you and your staff about what you do to attract visitors and what makes your museum more attractive than others." が依頼内容です。

**問3** Todd wants to  
**正解**: use pictures taken at the museum in his presentation.  
**解説**: "I would also like to ask for permission to take photos of the museum to use as slides in my presentation." が根拠です。

## 4B 説明文（Passage 9–15、各4問）

### Passage 9: Animals That Improve the Environment（ビーバー）

**問25** What happened to beavers in the past?  
**正解**: They decreased in many places due to human activities.  
**解説**: "In the past, the number of beavers became smaller because of human activities in various parts of the world." が根拠です。

**問26** In Riding Mountain National Park in Canada,  
**正解**: beavers help get rid of bad substances from the water.  
**解説**: "The dams also clean the water by removing harmful materials." が「悪い物質を水から取り除く」の言い換えです。

**問27** How do beavers have a positive impact on other wild animals?  
**正解**: They create homes suitable for other animals.  
**解説**: "their dams create places with healthier water... they make great homes for other wild animals" が根拠です。

**問28** Why do some people think beavers may have negative effects?  
**正解**: Because there is a chance that beavers may eat crops on farms.  
**解説**: "Some farmers think that there will be negative effects... This is because beavers may eat crops like corn on farms." が理由です。

### Passage 10: A Unique Italian Village（Chamois）

**問29** Which is correct to describe Chamois?  
**正解**: The village welcomes many visitors throughout the year.  
**解説**: "Many people visit the village throughout the year to enjoy the scenery and outdoor activities." が根拠です。

**問30** In the early 1960s, people in Chamois  
**正解**: needed to walk or ride a bicycle to visit other villages.  
**解説**: "People in Chamois used to travel by bicycle or on foot to visit other villages." が根拠です。

**問31** Why did the people of Chamois want cable cars?  
**正解**: To keep the nature of the village.  
**解説**: 道路建設に反対し、"Instead, they decided to build cable cars... while keeping the environment safe" とあるので、自然を守るためです。

**問32** What is true about Chamois today?  
**正解**: It is making its transportation system eco-friendly without cars.  
**解説**: 車を入れず、ケーブルカーで移動する「車を使わない環境に優しい交通」が本文の内容です。

### Passage 11: Salt

**問33** What did people think about salt in the past?  
**正解**: It had other purposes than adding flavor to meals.  
**解説**: "In the past, people thought about salt in a very different way. It had many more uses and was much more valuable." および保存・医薬・通貨などの記述が根拠です。

**問34** People in the past used salt as  
**正解**: a medicine to treat injured animals.  
**解説**: "Salt was also used in medicine. People used it to treat both humans and animals when they were sick or injured." が根拠です。

**問35** What is true about the impact of salt in the past?  
**正解**: New cities and roads were built to make or carry salt.  
**解説**: "In Europe, the salt trade helped cities grow and led to the building of new roads so that salt could be produced and carried from place to place." が根拠です。

**問36** How does salt help people's lives today?  
**正解**: It can be used as an item to clean things with.  
**解説**: "We also use it in new ways. For example, we use salt to clean and polish metal" が根拠です。

### Passage 15: Bees and the Environment

**正解**: They help plants make fruits and seeds through pollination.  
**解説**: "They help plants make fruits and seeds by carrying pollen from one flower to another. This process is called pollination." および "Many of the foods we eat... depend on bees" が根拠です。

**問50** What are scientists worried about?  
**正解**: The number of bees has been decreasing and it could affect our food supply.

**問51** What is one reason why bees are disappearing?

## まとめ

- **4A（Eメール）**: **誰が・何の目的で・何を依頼・提案しているか**、**理由・期限・代替案**を押さえる。選択肢の「言い換え」や「別の数字・日付」に注意。
- **4B（説明文）**: **段落ごとの事実・理由・比較**を追い、**Which is correct? / What is true about ...? / Why ...?** の根拠を本文の1〜2文で特定する。最後に主旨や「今日の状況」を問う問題にも対応できるようにする。
